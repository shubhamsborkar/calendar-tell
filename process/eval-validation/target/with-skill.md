**Where behavior disagrees with the street:** Management's behavior says "the turn has started" (raise, positive traffic, reinvesting); the street's says "show me a second quarter" — still Hold, avg target below price, Hold-heavy mix with a Sell tail. The twist is reversed from usual: **price has outrun the targets**, and Hold reads as the street being *behind* a one-quarter move it doesn't yet trust to repeat.

**One-line posture:** Confident on the fundamentals — first real guide raise and traffic inflection in over a year, new CEO owning the fix — but behaviorally restrained: doing the public-calendar minimum, hedged at the top by a retained predecessor, and running ahead of a still-skeptical Hold-rated street.
