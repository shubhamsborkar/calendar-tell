## What management guided on June 4, 2026

| | Q2 FY2026 guide | Full-year FY2026 guide |
|---|---|---|
| **Revenue** | $2.450B–$2.475B (**−3% to −2%**) | $11.00B–$11.15B (**−1% to flat**) |
| **Diluted EPS** | $1.76–$1.81 | **$10.95–$11.15** |

The full-year guide was **cut** from the March 17, 2026 guide of $11.35B–$11.50B
revenue and **$12.10–$12.30 EPS**; FY2026 EPS of ~$11 is a step down from FY2025's
**delivered $13.26**. Management attributed the cut to "negative commentary in the
media," disappointing product launches (the "Look of Yoga" / Away From Body line),
tariffs (280 bps Q1 hit), and fixed-cost deleverage.

## The full-year guide trajectory (the thing that's been moving)

Full-year EPS guide, quarter by quarter:

**$14.95–15.15** (Mar '25) → **$14.58–14.78** (Jun '25, CUT) → **$12.77–12.97**
(Sep '25, CUT) → **$12.92–13.02** (Dec '25, RAISED) → **delivered $13.26** →
then FY2026: **$12.10–12.30** (Mar '26) → **$10.95–11.15** (Jun '26, CUT).

That is **three full-year cuts and one raise** over the window, with FY2025
ultimately landing above its final (lowered) guide.
Sources: per-quarter company releases linked above; FY2024 starting guide from the
[Q4 FY2024 release](https://corporate.lululemon.com/media/press-releases/2025/03-27-2025-200544345).

## The read

Management consistently **beats the low bar it sets for the next quarter** (a
sandbag — EPS beat 5/5) while **repeatedly lowering the bar for the year** (three
FY cuts). The honest credibility signal is: *trust the short-term guide; treat the
full-year guide as the number that keeps moving down.* The new Q2 FY2026 guide is
probably another sandbag they clear — but the franchise itself deteriorated over
the period, and the latest quarter paired an EPS beat with a sharp full-year cut.
