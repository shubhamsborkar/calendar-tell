## The read (the internal contradictions)

- **Targets imply upside but the rating is Hold/"Reduce"** — only 2 buys against 27
  holds and 4 sells. When average targets sit 15–30% above the price yet nobody
  will say "buy," it means the targets are lagging indicators being walked down and
  the street doesn't yet trust the floor: *the price targets likely haven't
  finished falling.*
- **A real Sell tail.** Four explicit sells plus a downgrade to Underperform at $88
  is meaningful dispersion, not a uniformly constructive book.

This section reports the street's posture; it is **not** an endorsement of it, and
contains no rating or price target of the author's own.
